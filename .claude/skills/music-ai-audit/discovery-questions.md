# Discovery call — question bank

45 minutes, recorded with an AI note-taker (Fathom, Granola, or Otter). Goal: surface where the
owner loses hours, **without giving away the prescriptions live** — those go in the report. Listen
for repeated manual work, copy-paste, "I do this every week", and anything they dread.

Open by setting the frame: *"I'm going to ask about how work actually moves through your week. The
more honest you are about the annoying bits, the better the report. I'll send you a written plan
with the specific tools afterwards."*

## Base bank (any music business)

1. Walk me through a normal week. Where does the time actually go?
2. What's the first thing you do when you sit down, and how long does it take?
3. Which task do you dread most / keep putting off?
4. What do you do over and over that feels like it should be automatic?
5. How do you handle your inbox? Roughly how many emails a day, how long on it?
6. Where do things fall through the cracks — leads, follow-ups, deadlines?
7. What tools/apps/spreadsheets are you in every day? Which ones fight you?
8. When a new enquiry comes in, what happens? How fast do you reply? Who replies?
9. How do you keep track of contacts and relationships?
10. What do you copy-paste between tools by hand?
11. If you could hand one part of your week to an assistant tomorrow, what would it be?
12. Have you tried any AI tools? What happened?
13. How many hours a week would you guess you lose to admin you don't enjoy?

## ICP-specific sets (add 5–10 for this client)

### Indie label
- How do you run a release, start to finish — briefs, assets, metadata, delivery?
- How do you pitch to DSPs, playlists, press, radio? How much is manual research?
- How do you chase and store contacts (press, playlisters, radio, sync)?
- How do you track what's out, what's coming, and who owes you what?
- Metadata + asset packaging for distributors — how much is copy-paste?
- Royalty/statement admin — how painful, how often?

### Artist manager
- How much of your week is scheduling, chasing, and forwarding things?
- How do you keep the artist's calendar, deadlines, and deliverables straight?
- Contracts, splits, invoices — how are they handled?
- How do you brief collaborators (PR, producers, designers, venues)?
- How do you keep on top of the artist's inbox / DMs / opportunities?

### Music PR agency
- How do you build and clean media/contact lists for a campaign?
- How do you write and personalise pitches? How many per campaign, how long each?
- How do you track sends, opens, replies, and coverage?
- How do you report results to clients?
- (Note: deep campaign work maps to Total Audio Promo — flag as a warm handoff.)

### Studio / producer
- How do bookings and enquiries come in, and how fast do you reply?
- How do you handle scheduling, deposits, and invoicing?
- Client comms during and after a session — files, notes, revisions?
- How do you follow up to rebook past clients?

### Self-releasing artist
- How much time goes on promo admin vs actually making music?
- DSP/playlist/press outreach — how do you do it now?
- How do you keep track of contacts, submissions, and deadlines?
- Social content — how much is manual, how far ahead are you?

## Close the call

- "Anything I didn't ask that eats your time?"
- "What would 5 extra hours a week actually let you do?" (this becomes the ROI framing)
- Set expectations: *"You'll get the report in [X days], then we book 30 minutes to walk through it."*
